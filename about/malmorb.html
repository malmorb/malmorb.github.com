About Malmö.rb

What is Malmö.rb?

Malmö.rb – Malmö Ruby Brigade – is a group of Ruby enthusiasts about who meet regularly, to talk about technical things. Ruby is a programming language. Learn more about Ruby.

In late 2017, we restarted local Ruby community activity. Events have been organized roughly each month since then.

Is this for me?

If you're interested in meeting other people who program, or hearing about programming topics, this is a space to do that.

You don't have to bring anything, or have any experience, just curiosity.

What kinds of activities do you do?

Meetups have had prepared talks and presentations, things to learn. Most talks turn into discussions, with participants sharing stories and asking things to the whole group.

I haven't been to a meetup, what do I need to know?

If this is your first time, you may find the How To Malmö.rb page helpful.
